Click on either the EASY, MEDIUM or HARD buttons to start the game. Click RESET to restart. Take turns to click on the image on the right hand grid before the corresponding image and sound disapears on the left hand grid. Score points when you correctly click the correct image on the right before the image on the left disapears completely. If you click on the wrong image it will be removed from the grid and you will lose your turn.
